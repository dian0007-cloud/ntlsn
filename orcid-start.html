◇ Personalise · 30 seconds

Start with your ORCID.

Paste your ORCID iD and NTLSN reads your public scholarly record — right here in your browser — to build a starting point that's actually yours: a snapshot of your work, your SoTL footprint, and the commons resources most likely to help your teaching.

◌ Free, and genuinely private. We don't sell, on-sell, profile, or store your data — this is only to personalise your view of the commons.
  • The lookup runs entirely in your browser against ORCID's and OpenAlex's public APIs.
  • Your ORCID iD is never sent to NTLSN, never stored on our servers, never sold.
  • Nothing is saved unless you choose to — close the tab and it's gone.
  • Honest caveat: to look up your public record, your browser does send your iD to ORCID/OpenAlex. That's the only place it goes.
How the picks are chosen. We read your public works from OpenAlex (an open scholarly index), summarise your fields, and detect education- and teaching-related scholarship to estimate your SoTL footprint. Recommendations blend your field with universally useful practice — they're a starting point, not a verdict, and the quality depends on how complete your public record is. Crash courses are practitioner syntheses of sector good practice, honestly attributed on each page.

◇ AI field guide — before you paste your ORCID — notice the em-dashes (—)? The machine’s tell, and now yours to spot.
