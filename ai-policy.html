Unit AI-use policy builder.

"Can we use AI?" deserves a clearer answer than silence or a blanket ban. Set a level for each assessment — from no-AI to AI-integral — and we'll generate a student-facing policy you can paste straight into your unit outline. Per-task clarity is what protects integrity and trust.

🔴 No AI
Complete without generative AI.
🟠 Prep only
AI to explore; submission is your own.
🟢 With acknowledgement
AI as a tool, declared.
🔵 Integral
AI use is part of the task; show your process.
Per-task beats blanket. A whole-unit ban is unenforceable and pushes use underground; "anything goes" abandons integrity. Setting an explicit level per assessment — and saying how to disclose — is the approach Australian institutions and TEQSA are converging on. Align this with your university's academic-integrity policy; it complements, not replaces, it.
