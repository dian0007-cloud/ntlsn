What the School package adds

See your School, not just yourself

The free diagnostic maps each person's priorities; the School package aggregates them into one honest view of where your team is strong, where it isn't, and what to prioritise — the evidence base for a School plan.

Onboarding fit to your context

Induction tailored to your School's size, student cohort and delivery mode (online / on-campus / blended) — not a generic checklist — so new and sessional staff start strong.

Reporting that travels

School-level evidence you can take to accreditation, TEQSA and program review — mapped to recognised frameworks, drawn from the open commons.

Fund the commons, lead the sector

Your School's support keeps NTLSN free for every academic in the country — and positions your School as a founding contributor to a national teaching-and-learning commons.
