Viva / oral question generator.

A short oral conversation is one of the most AI-resilient ways to check that the person in front of you actually did — and understands — the work. Set the context and we'll generate a structured set of questions: warm-up, authenticity probes, understanding, and application.

Fairness is in the design. Ask each student a comparable spread of questions, mark against criteria they've seen, keep it short (5–10 min), and arrange accommodations in advance. A viva isn't a gotcha — it's a chance for students to show what they know in a way AI can't fake for them.
