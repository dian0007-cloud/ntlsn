Prompts for teaching with AI

Copy-paste prompts for the real work of teaching and the Scholarship of Teaching & Learning — designing assessment, aligning outcomes, giving feedback, reflecting on practice, and building a portfolio. Use them with any AI assistant. Fill the highlighted blanks, then copy.

Use with any assistant — and keep your judgement. These are starting points, not answers: always check what the AI gives back against your own expertise and your institution’s policy, and never paste identifiable student work into a public AI tool.
