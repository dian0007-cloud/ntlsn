Teaching promotion case builder.

A promotion case isn't a CV — it's an argument, organised by criteria and carried by evidence. Put your strongest claim and evidence against each of the seven teaching criteria, and a structured draft assembles on the right. Pairs with the AUTC self-check.

AB CDE ← level you're applying for
Triangulate, don't just assert. The strongest cases back each claim with at least two of: student feedback & outcomes, peer review of teaching, and reflective evidence of change over time. The chips track this for you — aim for evidence breadth, and let the scope of your impact rise to match the level you're seeking.
A free tool from NTLSN.com · structured to the Australian University Teaching Criteria & Standards (uniteachingcriteria.edu.au) · a drafting aid, not an official template · nothing leaves your browser.
