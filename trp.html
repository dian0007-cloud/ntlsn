Why a passport — and what's real here

Most teaching recognition is institution-locked: a Grad Cert, an induction, PD modules, internal awards — they often aren't re-credited when you move. The TRP keeps your record with you. This prototype holds self-entered records in your browser (nothing is sent anywhere). The full version issues verified, portable credentials through My eQuals — the platform Australian & New Zealand universities already use for official transcripts, testamurs and micro-credentials — so a teaching record from one university is trusted at another, and recognition finally travels across the 42. NTLSN doesn't reinvent credentialing (a light ~10-hour connection); it adds the teaching-recognition layer on top of My eQuals. Part of the proposed NTLSN platform · ntlsn.com
