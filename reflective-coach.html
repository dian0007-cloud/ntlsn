Reflective Coach

PROTOTYPE · runs in your browser · nothing is sent anywhere

A coach that asks the questions so you do the thinking. It walks you through your choice of seven leading reflective models, remembers your past sessions, and hands you back your own words, organised — ready for a teaching portfolio, an HEA claim, or just clearer practice.

Coach, never ghostwriter. Aletheia will not write your reflection for you. It mirrors, prompts and connects — the words, and the insight, stay yours.

⚙ Advanced — connect your own AI model (optional, off by default)

By default this coach uses a scripted Socratic engine — no AI, no internet, everything stays in this browser. If you turn this on, your typed text is sent only to the model endpoint you specify (your own key) to generate richer follow-up questions. This demonstrates Aletheia's model-agnostic design — DeepSeek, a local model, OpenAI, Anthropic, whatever you point it at.

off

Privacy: with this ON, your reflection text leaves your browser for the endpoint above. With it OFF (default), nothing is ever sent. Never paste student-identifying data into any AI tool.
