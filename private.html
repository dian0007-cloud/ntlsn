Your sector. Your data.
Your hardware.

A privacy-first option for the security-conscious: run NTLSN’s tools — and an open-source AI assistant — entirely on your own machine or campus. Nothing is sent to, or stored by, anyone. Built for individuals and schools who need genuine data sovereignty and a clean IT review.

Personalise it — two ways in

👤

For an individual

Download it; run it on your own laptop. The tools work offline. Add a local open-source model and your AI teaching coach runs on your machine — your drafts, your students’ work and your reflections never leave it.

🏫

For a school

Host the whole suite on one high-end on-prem machine. A single open-source LLM serves the school; IT keeps full control and can run it fully air-gapped. No cloud contracts, no data-residency questions, no per-seat AI bills.

How it stays private

  • Local-first tools Live today
    Every NTLSN “try it now” tool already runs entirely in your browser — open one and turn off your wifi; it still works. That is the foundation everything else builds on.
  • Your own open-source LLM In design
    Open models (e.g. Llama, Mistral, Qwen) run on your hardware via tools like Ollama or llama.cpp — no commercial AI API, no prompts or data leaving your network.
  • On-premise hosting In design
    Serve the suite from a local server or workstation; staff reach it on the campus network, or each person runs it locally. No public internet required.
  • Local storage, your files Live today
    Records stay on the device or your own server. You own the files; there is no NTLSN account and no server-side copy.
  • Air-gap capable In design
    For the most sensitive settings, it can run with no internet connection at all.
