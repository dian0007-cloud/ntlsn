Assessments · type & weight
The generative-AI exposure rating is a heuristic by task type — unsupervised text tasks (essays, reports, online quizzes) are generally easier to complete with AI than supervised, oral, practical or process-based tasks. It is a design prompt, not a verdict, and good task design can make any type more resilient. Aligns with NTLSN’s Calibration Suite and TEQSA’s assessment-reform principles.
