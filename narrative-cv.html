Narrative CV Builder

The narrative CV is the alternative to the publication-list CV: it asks what you've contributed and what difference it made — not how many citations in which journal. This builder scaffolds one for teaching scholarship, on the R4RI / Royal Society narrative model. Write in each module, watch your word count, and export. Everything stays in your browser.

Why a narrative CV DORA, the Leiden Manifesto and the Hong Kong Principles all argue assessment should weigh the contribution and its merit, not the venue or the count. A narrative CV makes that practical — it lets you show open practice, partnership, teaching, mentoring and service that a metrics CV renders invisible. Pair it with your SoTL Index for the evidence, and the RRA hub for the wider toolkit.

Frameworks & attribution. Module structure adapts the Résumé for Research and Innovation (R4RI) used by UKRI and the Royal Society narrative CV, with Glassick et al.'s (1997) standards for scholarly quality and DORA's principles. NTLSN is independent and not affiliated with these bodies. This is a drafting aid, not a template mandated by any institution — always check your own institution's or funder's required format and word limits. Runs entirely in your browser; nothing is stored or sent.
