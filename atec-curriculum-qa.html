Check a course before it ships.

Paste a course's outcomes and assessments. In your browser, this self-check looks at three things institutions now have to get right: do the outcomes match the AQF level, does the design show ATEC mission-compact alignment, and are the assessments resilient to generative AI.

⚠ A self-reflection aid, not an official TEQSA or ATEC assessment. The verb→AQF mapping and compact lexicons are illustrative and evidence-informed, not the regulator's determination — always work from the current HESF, AQF and your institution's processes. Nothing you type leaves your browser.
Part of the NTLSN open toolkit · free · no login · curation CC BY-NC-SA 4.0. Complements Constructive Alignment, Programme Map and Course Quality. ATEC = the Australian Tertiary Education Commission stewarding the HESF & mission-based compacts — this tool helps you self-check alignment before a course reaches committee.
