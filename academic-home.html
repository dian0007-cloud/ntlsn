Your academic development home

One ORCID iD, one home base. NTLSN turns your public record into a scholarly snapshot, scholarship-of-teaching ideas matched to your work, the Australian teaching-and-learning associations worth connecting with, and an editable view of the engagement and service that rarely counts in a workload model — then points you to the courses and tools to grow. Public data only; nothing is stored or sent.

Don't have one? Register a free iD at orcid.org. NTLSN reads only your public record, in your browser — it never sees a password and stores nothing.

A note on Canvas and other LMSs. NTLSN deliberately does not integrate with Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard or any LMS. Deep integration (LTI, institutional sign-on, gradebook access) means holding institutional credentials and student data — a security and privacy liability an independent, self-funded commons won't take on. Everything here runs in your browser, stores nothing, and sends nothing; you copy what's useful into your own systems. The human stays the integration layer.

Data, scope & independence. Profile data comes from your public ORCID record (CC0). Association descriptions are general and may change — always check the association's own site. Workload figures are rough, assumption-based prompts, not official load. This is an early prototype of an academic-development home base, not a finished platform.
