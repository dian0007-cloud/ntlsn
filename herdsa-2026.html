The program, threaded into the commons

Every session of the HERDSA 2026 on-site program — keynotes, papers and roundtables — clustered into the themes the commons already works in. Find your session, find your colleagues, and when the conference ends, keep each thread going on NTLSN: free, open, national.

An honest note. Sessions below are from the official on-site program (30 June 2026 release) and are subject to change — check the HERDSA conference app for rooms, times and late changes. Papers and roundtables are grouped here by theme, not by timetable. NTLSN is independent of HERDSA and of every institution named; listing implies no affiliation or endorsement.
