PSF 2023 Evidence Self-Audit

Map how well your evidence covers the 15 Dimensions of the Professional Standards Framework (PSF 2023) — across Areas of Activity, Core Knowledge and Professional Values. Rate each by how strongly you can evidence it. A coverage map to find where your evidence is thin — not a score, not a judgement of your practice.

A map, not a judgement. This helps you see where your evidence is strong or thin before you write — it does not assess your practice, score you, or determine a fellowship outcome.
