Portable Recognition Ledger

Turn your teaching recognition into a file you own — a standards-based Open Badges 3.0 / W3C Verifiable Credential, built from the open Recognition Credit Framework, with each achievement aligned to the PSF 2023, AQF, AAUT or HESF. Carry it anywhere; have it signed when issuance arrives.

Self-asserted, and standards-based — not crypto. This builds an unsigned draft credential you control. It uses Open Badges 3.0 / W3C Verifiable Credentials (the same standards behind My eQuals) — there is no blockchain, wallet or token. Cryptographic signing and verification (via My eQuals / an issuer) are on the NTLSN roadmap; until then every record is marked self-asserted. The file is built in your browser and never sent anywhere.
