What it is

A school symposium is the rare event that does four jobs at once: it inducts new and sessional staff into the school's teaching culture, delivers professional development through shared practice, surfaces work that's ready for recognition, and celebrates the people doing it. Symposium-in-a-box gives you everything to run one well — without it becoming a term's worth of admin.
