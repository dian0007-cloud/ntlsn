What this is — honestly

TELAS is ACODE's Technology Enhanced Learning Accreditation Standards; this self-check uses its 8 standards but is not a formal TELAS review or accreditation (that's a peer-reviewed process at telas.edu.au). UDL is CAST's Universal Design for Learning. Both run entirely in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere. On the NTLSN roadmap this becomes the quality-assurance layer (a TELAS API/SDK), complementary to ACODE — adds the self-check, never replaces the formal review.
