◇ The diagnostic · one review, every angle

The whole toolkit, in one review.

Most NTLSN tools look at one thing. This one looks at everything at once — and tells you which tools to reach for. Rate where you're at across the dimensions of good teaching, and get a prioritised action plan that routes you straight to the right tool for each gap. Works for a unit, a course, or a whole program.

A reflective self-review, not an audit or accreditation. Nothing leaves your browser. Each dimension links to the free NTLSN tool that goes deeper. Built on PSF 2023, AQF, TEQSA HESF, TELAS, UDL and AAUT criteria. CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.
