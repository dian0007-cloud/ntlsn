Learn SoTL from the sector itself

The sector has recorded hundreds of talks. These self-paced modules turn them into something you can learn from and finish: watch the talks on a theme, check your understanding, read the key ideas, reflect, and track what you've completed. Free, in-browser — your progress stays on your device.

📦 Add a module to your LMS. Open any module and hit Export (SCORM) — or use the badge on each card — to download a SCORM 1.2 package. Upload the .zip into Moodle, Canvas or Blackboard and it reports completion (and the quiz score) back to your gradebook.
