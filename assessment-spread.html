Assessment Spread Planner

List a unit's assessments and see where the load lands across the teaching weeks. Bunched deadlines are one of the most common — and most avoidable — causes of student stress. This shows you the shape of your schedule so you can spread it. Every assumption is visible; nothing is stored or sent.
